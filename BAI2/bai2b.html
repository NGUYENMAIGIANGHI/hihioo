Apple Bavarian Torte ★ ★ ★ ★

A classic European torte baked in a springform pan. Cream cheese, sliced almonds, and apples make this the perfect holiday treat (12 servings).

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 apples - peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. Cream together butter, sugar, vanilla, and flour.
  3. Press dough into the flat bottom of a 9-inch springform pan. Set aside.
  4. In a medium bowl, combine cream cheese, sugar, and egg. Beat on high until smooth. Pour cheese mixture over crust.
  5. Combine sliced apples, sugar, and cinnamon. Spread apple mixture over cream cheese mixture. Sprinkle almonds over top.
  6. Bake 10 minutes. Reduce heat to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) and continue baking for 25 minutes.
  7. Cool before removing from pan.
